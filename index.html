Welcome to your own personalized weather forcast!

Have you ever quickly looked at a weather app to see what to expect over the next couple days only to close out and still find yourself thinking, 'Wait- what do those predictions actually mean for me?'
Weather or Nah is going to show you how to use those temperature highs and lows to your advantage by providing a specialized 5 day forecast for the things that are important to you.
Select the events that are important to you, choose your desired temperature ranges for those events, then see your own personalized predictions!
